Welcome to the Heath AFM Lab research website


We perform and develop high-speed atomic force microscopy (HS-AFM) to study the dynamics of proteins, lipids and other biomolecules. HS-AFM is a powerful tool for studying the surface properties of biomaterials across lengthscales. Currently methods allow high-speed imaging at 70 frames per second, line scanning at milisecond timescales and single point recordings (Height Spectroscopy) at microseconds resolution.
